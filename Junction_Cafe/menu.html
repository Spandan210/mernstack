Breakfast Menu

Start your day with a delightful breakfast at Junction! Our breakfast menu is thoughtfully crafted to offer a variety of delicious and wholesome options, ensuring there's something for everyone. From classic favorites to innovative creations, our dishes are made using fresh, locally sourced ingredients to give you the perfect morning boost.

  • Classic Pancakes: Fluffy pancakes served with maple syrup, fresh berries, and a dollop of whipped cream.
  • Avocado Toast: Smashed avocado on toasted artisanal bread, topped with cherry tomatoes, radishes, and a sprinkle of sea salt.
  • Eggs Benedict: Poached eggs and Canadian bacon on a toasted English muffin, drizzled with our homemade hollandaise sauce.
  • Granola Parfait: Layers of creamy Greek yogurt, crunchy granola, and seasonal fruit, drizzled with honey.
  • Breakfast Burrito: A hearty burrito filled with scrambled eggs, cheese, black beans, and your choice of bacon or sausage, served with salsa and guacamole.
  • Healthy Smoothie Bowl: A refreshing blend of fruits and greens, topped with granola, chia seeds, and fresh fruit slices.
  • French Toast: Thick slices of brioche soaked in a cinnamon-vanilla batter, served with powdered sugar and fresh berries.

Lunch Menu

At Junction Cafe, we pride ourselves on offering a diverse and delectable main menu that caters to all tastes and preferences. Our dishes are crafted with the finest ingredients and a touch of culinary artistry, ensuring a delightful dining experience for every guest.

Drinks Menu

Quench your thirst and elevate your experience with our carefully curated drinks menu at Junction. Whether you're in the mood for a refreshing beverage, a comforting hot drink, or something with a bit of a kick, we have the perfect drink to complement your meal and satisfy your cravings.

  • Specialty Coffees: Enjoy our expertly brewed coffee, made from the finest beans sourced from around the world. Choose from classic espresso, creamy lattes, rich cappuccinos, and indulgent mochas.
  • Herbal and Traditional Teas: Savor the delicate flavors of our selection of teas, including green, black, and herbal varieties. Perfect for a moment of relaxation or an invigorating boost.
  • Smoothies and Fresh Juices: Rejuvenate with our vibrant smoothies and freshly squeezed juices, packed with natural goodness and available in a variety of fruity and veggie blends.
  • Iced Drinks: Cool down with our refreshing iced beverages, including iced coffee, iced tea, and specialty iced drinks that are perfect for a hot day.
  • Non-Alcoholic Beverages: Enjoy our variety of non-alcoholic options, such as sparkling water, soft drinks, and mocktails, all designed to provide a satisfying sip without the alcohol.
  • Craft Beers and Fine Wines: Choose from our range of locally brewed craft beers and a curated selection of fine wines. Whether you prefer a crisp white, a bold red, or a refreshing ale, we have the perfect pairing for your meal.
